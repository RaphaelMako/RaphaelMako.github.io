Hello, my name is Mako.

Growing up, I always felt so certain about the things going on in my life. But, over the past few years that certainty has faded away. I know I want to contribute to making the world a better place, but it’s hard to know where to start. The world is in constant flux and sometimes it seems impossible to make sense of any of it. This is why I’ve set out to write a blog.

One practice I’ve learned to do whenever uncertainty arises, is to solidify all the things I already know, and use that as a foundation to lead me forward. (Un)fortunately, in my uncertainty regarding life and the world as a whole, I fear I have set myself a monstrous goal of writing about everything - but what’s better for the human spirit than a project that never ends? As of right now, I have no explicit plan for how I’m going to organise my posts, and I’m sure the start will be a hodge podge of eclectic, disjointed, and maybe to some (although hopefully not to all), uninformed ideas. But, in an age where LLMs and endless content creation muddy our perceptions of the world, I hope to bore tunnels through the fog and help myself and any reader, eventually come to see a clearer, greater image of life.

Mako
